Apolinario Mabini: The Sublime Paralytic

Background: Apolinario Mabini, often referred to as "The Sublime Paralytic," was born on July 23, 1864, in Tanauan, Batangas, Philippines. Bonifacio founded the Katipunan (Kataas-taasan, Kagalang-galangang Katipunan ng mga Anak ng Bayan), a secret revolutionary society aimed at gaining independence from Spain through armed revolt. The Katipunan played a pivotal role in mobilizing Filipinos to join the revolution.
